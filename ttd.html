TTD Special Darshan Information

300/- darshan tickets or Special Entry is given for advance reservation to take the darshan in the specified slot. Many people tried to book on the spot, but now 120 days advance booking is allowed on ttdsevaonline.com website. The official website offers the best chances to get the tickets with minimum effort.

TTD is releasing in bulk 300 rupees special darshan quota for upcoming months of April 2024. The online website home page is updated with the latest schedule to book tickets.

Srivari Darshan quota for April month is to be released on 24 January (Wednesday) online at 10:00 AM Sharp.
